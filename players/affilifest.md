## Overview

Affilifest is a UK-based affiliate and partnerships marketing conference series that has grown into one of the most talked-about industry gatherings in the European affiliate space. Unlike traditional conference formats that charge significant ticket fees and lean heavily toward vendor showcase floor space, Affilifest takes a deliberately different approach: admission is completely free for brands, retailers, and publishers, with the event funded primarily by network and platform sponsors.

The model creates a different attendee mix — affiliate managers, in-house performance marketing teams, and publisher-side operators who might otherwise be priced out of industry conferences. This positions Affilifest as a community-first gathering rather than a sales event, which has resonated strongly with practitioners looking for genuine peer learning and deal-making opportunities.

In 2026, Affilifest is running as a two-event series: **Affilifest South** at Dreamland, Margate (June 3–4, 2026) and **Affilifest North** in a separate UK location. The conference's 2026 agenda was announced April 29, just 3 days ago, and is focused on practical responses to the industry's current pressures: AI disruption, creator commerce integration, incrementality measurement, and the blurring of affiliate channels with content and social commerce.

## How It Works

Affilifest operates as a traditional industry conference with a few structural differentiators:

1. **Free brand/publisher access**: Brands, retailers, and publishers attend at no cost. This expands the attendee base and creates a better buyer-to-seller ratio than many industry events.
2. **Sponsor-funded model**: Networks, platforms, agencies, and technology vendors pay to sponsor, exhibit, and present. This is the revenue model that subsidizes free attendee access.
3. **Curated agenda**: The agenda is built around practical affiliate and partnerships management topics — not product pitches. Sessions address real challenges affiliate managers face in 2026.
4. **Networking and deal-making**: Designed for publishers, brands, and networks to meet and establish new partnerships in a concentrated setting.
5. **Speaker community**: Open to fresh voices from across the affiliate and partnership space — not just established keynote speakers.

## Pricing Model

**For brands, retailers, publishers**: **Free admission** (registration required)  
**For sponsors/exhibitors**: Paid packages (pricing negotiated with Affilifest commercial team)  
**For speakers**: Open application process, no speaking fees cited

The free-to-attend model is the most distinctive element of Affilifest's value proposition for the brand/publisher side.

## Strengths

- **Free access democratizes participation**: Brands and publishers who can't justify high conference costs can still participate, creating a broader and more diverse attendee base.
- **Practitioner-focused programming**: Sessions are built around real affiliate management challenges rather than vendor showcase time.
- **UK/European affiliate community hub**: Fills a gap left by US-centric events (Affiliate Summit, PI LIVE) as a dedicated UK/European practitioner gathering.
- **Impact.com sponsorship / industry credibility**: Being listed as an official Impact.com event partner signals strong industry support.
- **Growing community signals**: Top Women in Affiliate recognition programs and active blog content building year-round engagement beyond the event itself.
- **Fresh voice programming**: Open speaker applications welcome emerging voices, not just established names.

## Weaknesses

- **UK/Europe regional focus**: Limited relevance for US-focused Flywheel vendors unless they operate in European markets.
- **Sponsor-funded model constraints**: Content must balance practitioner value with sponsor visibility, which can create implicit pressure toward network/platform perspectives.
- **Smaller scale than Affiliate Summit**: Compared to Affiliate Summit East (4,500+ attendees), Affilifest operates at a more intimate scale.
- **Less established brand outside UK**: While growing, Affilifest lacks the 20+ year brand recognition of Affiliate Summit in the global affiliate consciousness.

## Industry Context

Affilifest is part of a broader diversification of the affiliate industry conference calendar, which historically was dominated by Affiliate Summit (US-focused) and PI LIVE (London). New events like Affilifest have emerged to serve specific community segments — in this case, UK/European practitioners who want practitioner-focused, accessible content without US-centric bias or expensive ticket prices.

**Comparable events:**
- **Affiliate Summit East/West** — US, largest scale, paid ticket, broader scope
- **PI LIVE** — London, established UK event, more enterprise/network focus
- **Affilifest** — UK, free admission, practitioner-first, community-driven
