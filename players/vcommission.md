## Overview

vCommission is India's largest performance marketing and affiliate network, founded in 2010 and headquartered in New Delhi. While deeply rooted in the Indian market, vCommission operates as a genuinely global network with offices and operations in Singapore, UAE, UK, and USA — positioning it as the premier affiliate gateway for brands seeking to reach South Asian, Southeast Asian, and Middle Eastern consumer markets, where affiliate-driven e-commerce is growing substantially faster than Western markets.

The network connects advertisers across e-commerce, travel, utilities, lead generation, and cash-on-delivery (COD) e-commerce with over 100,000 affiliates worldwide. Unlike traditional Western affiliate networks that started in display/content and expanded into e-commerce, vCommission was purpose-built around performance marketing in high-growth mobile-first markets — giving it structural advantages in mobile tracking, COD attribution (critical in India where cash payment remains dominant), and localized advertiser relationships with major regional platforms like Myntra, Nykaa, Shopee, Meesho, and HK Vitals.

Founded by Tarang Bhargava (Founder) and Parul Tarang Bhargava (Co-Founder & CEO), vCommission has quietly become a significant force in global affiliate marketing while remaining less visible in Western industry conversations — making it a meaningful discovery gap for brands building international affiliate strategies.

## How It Works

vCommission operates as a traditional performance-based affiliate network, functioning as the intermediary between advertisers and publishers:

1. **Advertiser Onboarding**: Brands join vCommission and configure their affiliate program — setting commission rates, creative assets, tracking parameters, and geographic targeting.
2. **Publisher Network Access**: vCommission's 100,000+ affiliates include content publishers, coupon and cashback sites, social media influencers, comparison sites, and performance-focused traffic sources across mobile and desktop.
3. **Real-Time Tracking**: vCommission's proprietary tracking infrastructure monitors clicks and conversions in real-time, with a 7-day cookie window and support for web, mobile app, and COD conversion tracking.
4. **Optimization & Reporting**: Advertiser and publisher dashboards provide campaign-level performance data, with account management support for major programs.
5. **Payment Processing**: Regular commission payouts to publishers with international payment capabilities across multiple currencies.

### The COD E-Commerce Vertical

One of vCommission's most distinctive capabilities is its "India COD Ecom" vertical — a dedicated infrastructure for cash-on-delivery affiliate campaigns. COD remains the dominant payment method for e-commerce purchases in India (representing 50%+ of transactions in many categories), and most Western affiliate networks lack the tracking architecture to properly attribute COD conversions. vCommission's native COD affiliate tracking is a genuine competitive advantage for brands entering the Indian market.

Two distinct lead generation verticals — one serving the Indian market (financial services, insurance, education, real estate) and one serving US advertisers — with separate publisher pools optimized for each geography.

Dedicated affiliate onboarding, training, and growth support managed by the Director of Affiliate Growth team. Focus on publisher quality, compliance, and scale.

### Advertiser Growth Services
Global and India-specific advertiser growth teams that actively help brands expand their publisher footprint, optimize commission structures, and enter new regional markets.

## Pricing & Commission Model

vCommission operates on the traditional network model:

- **Commission Rates**: Vary by vertical and advertiser. E-commerce programs range from 2-14%, with travel and financial services typically higher.
- **Cookie Duration**: Standard 7-day cookie window across programs.
- **Network Override**: vCommission charges advertisers a network fee on top of publisher commissions, consistent with standard affiliate network practice. Exact rates are negotiated per program.
- **Publisher Payout**: Publishers receive commissions directly from vCommission upon conversion validation. Payout frequency and methods vary by publisher tier and geography.

## Strengths

- **India Market Depth**: No Western affiliate network comes close to vCommission's publisher depth and advertiser relationships in India. For brands entering the Indian market, vCommission is the default affiliate partner.
- **COD Attribution**: Unique infrastructure for cash-on-delivery conversion tracking — a capability that is genuinely essential for Indian e-commerce and essentially unavailable elsewhere.
- **Multi-Region Footprint**: Offices in India, Singapore, UAE, UK, and USA mean genuine on-the-ground support in each market, not just a website claiming global reach.
- **Mobile-First Architecture**: Built for mobile-first markets from the ground up, with strong mobile app tracking and mobile publisher relationships.
- **High-Growth Market Access**: The markets vCommission serves — India, Southeast Asia, Middle East — are growing substantially faster than the US and European affiliate markets. The network is positioned in the highest-growth zones of global e-commerce.
- **100,000+ Affiliate Scale**: Publisher breadth creates a broad distribution network across content types (coupons, cashback, comparison, influencer, social) in its core markets.
- **Active Content Production**: vCommission maintains an active research blog covering market trends, best offers, and publisher optimization strategies — a signal of an engaged, publisher-focused organization.

## Weaknesses

- **Limited Western Brand Recognition**: vCommission is not well-known in US and European affiliate circles, which can create friction for global brands whose affiliate teams operate from Western headquarters.
- **Reduced Transparency on Scale Metrics**: Unlike US networks that regularly publish transaction volume and GMV data, vCommission does not prominently disclose financial performance metrics, making external validation of claimed scale difficult.
- **Technology Gap vs. SaaS Platforms**: vCommission's platform is a traditional network dashboard rather than a modern SaaS product, which may feel dated compared to Impact.com, Everflow, or Partnerize.
- **Compliance and Fraud Risk**: Operating in markets with high coupon and incentive traffic volume requires strong publisher compliance infrastructure. The quality of this compliance is harder to assess from the outside for Western brands not already operating in these markets.
- **English-Language Coverage**: While vCommission serves global advertisers, the bulk of its publisher base operates in Indian languages (Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, etc.), which requires market-specific content strategy for advertisers.

## Market Position & Competitive Context

vCommission occupies a unique geographic niche in global affiliate marketing — it is the undisputed leader in India, with no close competitor on publisher depth or COD infrastructure:

- **vs. Admitad**: Admitad is a Russian-founded global performance network with strong Eastern European and some Asian market presence. vCommission is stronger in South Asia specifically.
- **vs. CJ/Awin/Rakuten in India**: Western networks have India-facing programs but lack vCommission's publisher depth, COD capability, and regional account management. Most large Indian brands use vCommission as their primary affiliate partner, with Western networks as secondary.
- **vs. mGid, PropellerAds**: These are performance networks with Asian presence but focused on display and push traffic rather than the content-affiliate model vCommission emphasizes.

For Western brands: vCommission is best understood as a regional specialist partner rather than a full-service global network replacement. It is the right tool for India market entry and Southeast Asian affiliate expansion, used alongside (not instead of) a primary Western network.
