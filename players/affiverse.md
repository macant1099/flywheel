## Overview

Affiverse (formerly Affiliate Insider) is the United Kingdom's leading affiliate industry media, training, and agency platform. Founded in 2018 by performance marketing veteran Lee-Ann Johnstone, the company has grown into what it claims is the #1 destination for affiliate marketing news globally, with a strong readership among affiliate managers, performance marketers, publishers, and agency professionals.

The company operates at the intersection of media, education, and consulting — publishing daily editorial content covering industry trends, regulatory developments, platform updates, and strategy, while also offering structured training programs for affiliate professionals and managed agency services for brands seeking to launch or grow affiliate programs.

In early 2026, Affiverse announced an evolution of its agency brand positioning itself as a "next era performance marketing" agency, reflecting the company's response to AI-driven and creator-commerce shifts reshaping the industry.

## What It Does

Affiverse operates across three core business lines:

### Media (affiversemedia.com)
Daily news coverage, feature articles, interviews with industry leaders, opinion pieces, and event coverage for the affiliate and performance marketing community. Topics include affiliate network and platform developments, regulatory updates (FTC, ASA), AI disruption of the affiliate channel, creator commerce trends, and brand strategy. The publication is widely read across UK, US, and European affiliate markets.

### Training (AMPP — Affiliate Marketing Practitioner Program)
Structured learning programs for affiliate managers and marketing professionals looking to build or advance their performance marketing skills. Training covers affiliate program setup, publisher recruitment, compliance, fraud prevention, optimization strategy, and creator commerce integration.

### Agency Services
Affiverse operates as an affiliate marketing agency for brands, offering program launches, publisher recruitment, program audits, and ongoing program management. The agency side has expanded in 2026 under a repositioned brand focused on "next-era performance marketing."

### Events
Affiverse runs or supports industry events and conferences focused on affiliate and performance marketing, with a particular emphasis on UK and European markets.

Based on recent editorial output, Affiverse is tracking these major themes:

- **Creator Commerce**: The convergence of influencer marketing and affiliate performance, particularly Instagram and TikTok developments
- **Publisher Diversification**: Helping publishers shift from organic-traffic-dependent models to paid and multi-channel revenue strategies
- **Compliance & Fraud Prevention**: FTC enforcement actions, consumer review compliance, and fraud detection best practices
- **Platform Shifts**: Coverage of major moves like the Rakuten/Impact.com alliance, Awin/ShareASale migration, and Meta's affiliate commerce expansion

## Industry Position

Affiverse occupies a unique position as a trusted, independent media voice in the affiliate industry. Unlike network or platform-owned media, it is not beholden to any single technology provider or network, giving it credibility across the ecosystem. It serves as a primary intelligence source for affiliate professionals navigating a rapidly changing landscape.

Key audience segments:
- In-house affiliate and performance marketing managers at brands
- Affiliate publishers and content creators seeking industry intelligence
- Agency professionals managing affiliate programs
- Platform and network business development teams

## Strengths

- **Independent Voice**: Not owned by a network or platform, ensuring editorial independence
- **Daily Publishing Cadence**: Consistent, high-volume editorial output keeps the audience engaged
- **Community Building**: Strong community following among UK and international affiliate professionals
- **Training Revenue**: AMPP training program creates recurring revenue beyond advertising
- **Agency Credibility**: Positioning as practitioner-led agency distinguishes from pure consultancies
- **AI/Creator Coverage**: Strong editorial investment in the biggest industry disruptions of 2025-2026

## Weaknesses

- **UK-Centric Roots**: While covering global markets, roots are in UK affiliate industry, which may limit US brand awareness
- **Revenue Concentration**: Heavy reliance on media advertising and sponsorship from the networks/platforms it covers
- **Scale**: Compared to US-based trade publications, Affiverse is smaller in reach and revenue
- **Agency vs. Media Tension**: Operating both as agency and editorial creates potential conflicts of interest
