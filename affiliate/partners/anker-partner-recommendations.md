## Strategic Recommendations

### Quick Wins (Execute in 30 days)

1. **Audit existing Amazon Associates leakage.** Many warm leads (9to5Toys, CNN Underscored, MacRumors) are likely monetizing Anker traffic through Amazon Associates at 3% rather than Anker's direct 8% program. Run an Impact report to identify which publishers are NOT in Anker's direct program, then send targeted recruitment emails highlighting the commission differential (8% vs. 3%).

2. **Create partner-specific promo codes.** Generate unique discount codes for top warm leads (TechRadar, Tom's Guide, CNN Underscored) to incentivize direct link placement over Amazon routing. Example: "TECHRADAR10" for 10% off, tracked via Impact.

3. **Establish review unit pipeline.** Set up a standing quarterly product seeding program for the top 10 partners. Ensure every new product launch includes embargoed review units to all priority partners simultaneously.

### Medium-Term (60-90 days)

4. **International expansion push.** Connect UK/EU publications (Trusted Reviews, other European tech sites) to Anker's regional affiliate programs on Webgains (8-15% Nordics) and Impact (7% UK, 8% EU-DE). The regional programs offer competitive or better terms than the US program.

5. **YouTube creator tier.** Establish a dedicated YouTube creator commission tier within Impact (or via SuperChargers program expansion) with 10% commission and exclusive promo codes. Target JerryRigEverything, ZolloTech, and Jonathan Morrison as initial partners.

6. **Full-portfolio affiliate strategy.** Most partners currently cover Anker chargers/power banks only. Create dedicated affiliate playbooks for AnkerWork (webcams, speakers), Soundcore (audio), Nebula (projectors), and SOLIX (solar/power stations) to expand coverage across the full brand portfolio.

### Long-Term (90-180 days)

7. **AI agent affiliate program.** Develop agent-compatible affiliate feeds and API access for AI shopping tools (ChatGPT Shopping, Google AI, Perplexity). This addresses the critical gap identified in the AFARE scorecard (1/4 on AI Agent Affiliate Presence).

8. **Membership affiliate integration.** When Anker launches a membership/subscription offering, create affiliate-trackable signup events with recurring commission on renewals. This enables partners to promote ongoing value rather than one-time purchases.
